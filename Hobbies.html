Jessica's Hobbies

About Hobbies

Reading

Jessica is seriously into adult fantasy fiction novels. Though school keeps her busy, she still manages to find time to escape into the otherworldly adventures of her favorite authors, particularly those by Sarah J. Maas.

How much reading does she do? Last year alone, she worked her way through over 150 books!

Mobile Gaming

To unwind from her hectic academic schedule, Jessica really enjoys mobile gaming, in particular Stardew Valley which lets her farm, explore, and build relationships from the comfort of her couch.

For Jessica, gaming is like a mini-vacation for the mind. It allows her to unplug from the demands of her studies and recharge her batteries.

Photography

As an aspiring photographer, Jessica is currently working on creating her first Pixieset site. What started with family and friends has expanded to clientele in her local community, and even to the plains of Africa for some wildlife shoots!

Through her lens, Jessica finds great value in exploring the world from different perspectives.
