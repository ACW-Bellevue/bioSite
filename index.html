About Hobbies

Civil Service

A veteran of the U.S. Navy, Jessica served on 2 overseas deployments and multiple training detatchments as an F/A-18 strike fighter squadron aircraft mechanic. Her resume also includes work with various other federal agencies, as well as housing programs for the homeless.

Academics

Jessica holds a bachelor's degree in clinical psychology and master's degree in forensic psychology from Cal Baptist University, a masters degree in clinical psychology from Loma Linda university, and is currently at Loma Linda pursuing a PsyD in neuropsychology, which she's looking to complete by 2026.

Goals

Jessica is particularly interested in leveraging her background and education to enhance mental health accessibility and eventually serve the veteran community. She likes the idea of utilizing her past military experience to connect with and assist soldiers in need of psychological services.
